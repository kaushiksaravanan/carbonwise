# 🗺️ CarbonWise Roadmap

> Building the most delightful, privacy-first personal carbon tracker — one small habit at a time.

---

## 🚀 v1.0 (Current — June 2026)

The foundation: a personal, AI-powered carbon coach that lives in your pocket.

- ✅ Onboarding quiz with lifestyle profiling
- ✅ Daily carbon budget with visual ring
- ✅ AI Carbon Coach via Gemini
- ✅ What-If simulator
- ✅ Living digital garden
- ✅ Weekly insights
- ✅ Privacy-first (localStorage)

---

## 🎯 v1.5 — Engagement (Q3 2026)

Make the daily check-in feel rewarding. Turn awareness into momentum.

- 📊 **Achievement badges** — first tree planted, 7-day streak, 100kg CO₂ saved
- 🌍 **Global & regional comparisons** — see how you stack up against your country/city
- 📱 **PWA support** — installable, offline-first, home-screen icon
- 🔔 **Smart reminders** — gentle nudges after meals and at end of day
- 📤 **Shareable garden screenshots** — flex your forest on social media

---

## 🤝 v2.0 — Community (Q4 2026)

Carbon reduction is a team sport. Bring friends in — without sacrificing privacy.

- 👥 **Optional social layer** — friend leaderboards and group challenges
- 🏆 **Carbon Heroes board** — weekly anonymous, opt-in community spotlight
- 🌱 **Plant a real tree** — partner with [One Tree Planted](https://onetreeplanted.org)
- 💬 **AI Coach personas** — pick your vibe: enthusiast, skeptic, or scientist

---

## 🔌 v2.5 — Integrations (Q1 2027)

Stop typing, start tracking automatically. Meet users where their data already lives.

- 🏠 **Smart home integrations** — Nest, Ecobee for automatic energy tracking
- 🚗 **GPS-based commute detection** — auto-log transport CO₂
- 🍴 **Receipt scanning** — food and grocery emission tracking via OCR
- 💳 **Bank connections** — purchase-based emission estimation (opt-in, encrypted)

---

## 🌐 v3.0 — Carbon Marketplace (2027)

Close the loop: from awareness to measurable, verified climate action.

- 💸 **Verified carbon offset purchases** — Gold Standard & Verra-certified projects
- 🏪 **Eco-product recommendations** — tailored to your real consumption patterns
- 📈 **Yearly carbon report card** — beautiful, shareable PDF summary

---

## 🛠️ Technical Debt & Improvements

Ongoing work to keep the foundation strong as we scale.

- 🧪 Add E2E tests with **Playwright**
- ⚡ Migrate to App Router **server components** where possible
- 🌏 Add **i18n** support — starting with Hindi and Spanish
- 📚 Expand the **emission factors database** with region-specific data
- 📴 Add a **service worker** for true offline support

---

## 🌟 Vision

> **Make personal carbon tracking as easy and habitual as checking your step count.**

Climate action shouldn't require a spreadsheet, a guilt trip, or a PhD. It should fit in 30 seconds a day, feel a little magical, and add up to something real over a year.

That's CarbonWise.
